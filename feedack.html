Water Saving Methods at Home

Every drop counts! Here are some easy tips to save water at home:

  • Take shorter showers (aim for 5 minutes or less).
  • Fix leaky faucets and toilets promptly.
  • Water your lawn and garden only when necessary, early morning or evening to reduce evaporation.
  • Use a broom instead of a hose to clean outdoor areas.
  • Install low-flow showerheads and faucet aerators.
